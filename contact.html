Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to purchase a vehicle?

To purchase a vehicle, you'll need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and proof of income if financing. If you're trading in a vehicle, bring your title and registration as well.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer a variety of financing options to fit different budgets and credit situations. Our finance team works with multiple lenders to find the best rates and terms for you.

Can I schedule a test drive online?

Yes, you can schedule a test drive through our website or by contacting us directly via phone, email, or WhatsApp. We recommend scheduling in advance to ensure the vehicle is available.

Do your vehicles come with a warranty?

Many of our vehicles come with the remainder of the manufacturer's warranty. We also offer extended warranty options for additional peace of mind. Ask our sales team about warranty options for specific vehicles.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 3-day/300-mile return policy on most vehicles. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within this period for a full refund or exchange. Some conditions apply, so please ask for details.
